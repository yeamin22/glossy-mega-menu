The WordPress Easy Website Form Plugin is a user-friendly tool designed to help website owners create and manage custom forms.

== Description ==
The WordPress Easy Website Form Plugin is an essential tool for website owners and administrators looking to enhance their website\'s functionality and user engagement without delving into the complexities of coding. This plugin simplifies the creation and management of custom forms, including contact forms, registration forms, surveys, and feedback forms, through an intuitive drag-and-drop interface, making it accessible to users of all skill levels.

Key Features:

Drag-and-Drop Form Builder: Allows easy form creation by simply dragging and dropping form fields into place, making it possible to design complex forms in minutes.
Pre-Designed Form Templates: Offers a variety of customizable templates to kickstart your form creation process, catering to diverse needs and saving valuable time.
Mobile-Responsive Design: Ensures that forms look great and function flawlessly on devices of all sizes, providing a seamless experience for users on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
Advanced Field Types: Supports a wide range of field types, including text, email, dropdowns, checkboxes, and file uploads, accommodating various data collection requirements.
Conditional Logic: Enables dynamic forms that change based on user input, allowing for a personalized and efficient form filling experience.
Spam Protection: Integrates powerful anti-spam techniques, including CAPTCHA and honeypot methods, to keep forms secure and spam-free.
Email Notifications: Automatically sends email notifications to administrators upon form submission, ensuring prompt responses to inquiries or feedback.
Integration Capabilities: Seamlessly integrates with popular email marketing services and CRM software, facilitating efficient data management and marketing efforts.
Whether you\'re looking to gather customer feedback, increase engagement through surveys, or streamline your contact processes, the WordPress Easy Website Form Plugin offers a comprehensive solution that combines ease of use with powerful functionality. By incorporating this plugin into your WordPress site, you can improve your website\'s interactivity, enhance user satisfaction, and gather valuable insights with minimal effort.

== Installation ==
Download easywebsiteform.zip 
Unzip & Upload the easywebsiteform folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
Activate the easywebsiteform from plugins.
Go to the easywebsiteform setup page and use your api key for using the forms.
